# Level A1 — Lesson One: Greetings and Introductions

> Source language: Egyptian Colloquial Arabic. Transliterations are approximate.

## Lesson Objectives

By the end of the lesson, the learner can:

- Give a greeting in an appropriate way.
- Respond to a greeting.
- Introduce themselves simply.
- Ask for the other person's name.
- Recognize some basic courtesy phrases.

## Practice

### Dialogue (Listening)

> **Ahmed:** Good morning.
> **Ibrahim:** Good morning (response).
> **Ahmed:** How are you?
> **Ibrahim:** Thank God.
> **Ahmed:** My name is Ahmed, and you?
> **Ibrahim:** I'm Ibrahim.
> **Ahmed:** Welcome, Ibrahim.
> **Ibrahim:** I'm a new colleague here.
> **Ahmed:** Welcome — you light up the place.
> **Ibrahim:** Bless you (lit. "may God keep you").
> **Ahmed:** I'm glad I got to know you.
> **Ibrahim:** Me too.
> **Ahmed:** Okay, I'll leave you so I can make it to the lecture.
> **Ibrahim:** Okay, go ahead.
> **Ahmed:** Bye.
> **Ibrahim:** Bye.

### Dialogue (Reading)

**Dialogue 1**

> **Ahmed:** Good morning.
> **John:** Good morning (response).
> **Ahmed:** What is your name?
> **John:** My name is John.
> **Ahmed:** Nice to meet you.
> **John:** Me too.

**Dialogue 2**

> **Mona:** How are you?
> **Sara:** Thank God, I'm fine.
> **Mona:** How are things?
> **Sara:** All good.

### Dialogue (Speaking)

> What is your name? — My name is Mohamed.
> How are you? — Fine, thank God.
> Where are you from? — I'm from Egypt.

## Activities

### Activity (Reading)

> Hello. My name is Ahmed. I'm from Egypt. I'm a student. Welcome, everyone.

**Questions**

- What is the speaker's name? ...................
- Which country is he from? ...................
- What does he do? ...................

### Activity (Listening)

> Good evening. My name is Mariam. I'm a teacher from Egypt. Nice to meet you all.

- What is the speaker's name? ...................
- Which country is she from? ...................
- What does she do? ...................

### Activity (Speaking)

**Activity 1:** Conduct a short interview with your classmate:

- What is your name? ................................
- Where are you from? ................................
- How are you? ................................

**Activity 2:** Introduce yourself in three sentences:

- My name is ...........
- I am from ...........
- I am a/an ................

### Listening Activity (Additional Suggestion)

Listen to the following dialogue, then answer:

> **Ahmed:** Hello.
> **Ali:** Welcome.
> **Ahmed:** How are you?
> **Ali:** Thank God, fine.

**Comprehension Questions**

- What did Ahmed say first?
- How did Ali respond?
- How was Ali doing?

## Final Assessment

The learner is asked to conduct a one-minute dialogue that includes:

- A greeting.
- Asking for the name.
- Asking how someone is doing.
- Asking about the country.
- Ending the conversation in an appropriate way.
